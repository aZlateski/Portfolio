About
Who am i ?

Hi, I am Aleksandar Z. My attitude to seek creativity in everyday life and the strong will to master visual communication eventually blended together in the Frontend developer role, my current profession. My long-term dedication to Visual communication, was crowned with a degree in Web Development from a respective academy in 2021, after having graduated from a B.Sc. in software engineering in 2011 and Graphic Design Academy in 2015.

I seek to use my creativity, technical skill and marketing proficiency to generate visually appealing solutions. I expertly utilize Web Design softwares and tools to bring ideas to life giving the client unique and immersive web experience. I also engage in frequent market research to accurately determine preferences of target customers.

I enjoy collaborating with clients, creative teams and production professionals to deliver design solutions propelling web traffic, social media engagement, response rates and customer-acquisition results. Because of the multi-disciplinary character of the industry I have managed to successfully combine the fields of Web Design , Web Development and User Experience as the key principles of a successful digital product.
